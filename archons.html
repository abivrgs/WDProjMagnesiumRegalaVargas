ARCHONS

Archons are the divine rulers of Teyvat, each governing a specific region and element. They shape the world through their power, wisdom, and vision, inspiring mortals while guiding their nations through history and destiny.

Archon Region Element

Venti
Venti is the playful Anemo Archon of Mondstadt, known for his love of freedom and music. He inspires the people through his songs and wind-guided wisdom.

Despite his carefree personality, he holds deep knowledge about Mondstadt’s history and legends, often appearing when the city needs guidance.

 Mondstadt Anemo

Zhongli
Zhongli is the Geo Archon of Liyue, revered for his wisdom and mastery over contracts. He guides Liyue with patience and a deep understanding of history.

Though he often walks among mortals in disguise, his presence is always felt, and his actions quietly shape the prosperity of Liyue.

 Liyue Geo

Raiden Ei
The Electro Archon of Inazuma, Raiden Ei enforces eternity while balancing her nation’s traditions and innovations. She wields the mighty Electro Archon powers with precision.

Her rule is marked by discipline and order, and she watches over Inazuma with unwavering dedication to her vision of eternity.

 Inazuma Electro

Nahida
Nahida is the Dendro Archon of Sumeru, representing wisdom and knowledge. She encourages intellectual growth and reverence for the mysteries of nature.

She is gentle and thoughtful, guiding scholars and citizens alike with her insights into the natural world and the secrets it holds.

 Sumeru Dendro

Furina
Furina, the Hydro Archon of Fontaine, oversees law and justice in her watery realm. She is cunning, strategic, and deeply committed to her domain.

Behind her composed demeanor lies a sharp mind, capable of orchestrating complex plans to maintain order and balance in Fontaine.

 Fontaine Hydro

Mavuika
The Pyro Archon of Natlan, Mavuika embodies passion, freedom, and fiery spirit. He inspires the people with his courage and energetic presence.

He is a symbol of courage and rebellion, always encouraging those around him to embrace their inner fire and pursue their dreams boldly.

 Natlan Pyro

Tsaritsa
Tsaritsa is the Cryo Archon of Snezhnaya. This region has not yet been released in-game, but she governs with elegance and cold authority.

Her ambitions are mysterious, and she quietly influences events across Teyvat to achieve her goals, showing that even quiet power can be formidable.

 Snezhnaya (unreleased) Cryo
