ELEMENTS

Pyro

Pyro is associated with Murata, the Pyro Archon of Natlan, and represents fire, passion, and raw strength. In gameplay, Pyro triggers powerful damage-focused reactions such as Vaporize, Melt, Overloaded, and Burning. Symbolically, Pyro stands for ambition, conflict, and transformation, making it one of the strongest offensive elements.

Cryo

Cryo is ruled by the Tsaritsa, the Cryo Archon of Snezhnaya, and embodies cold, restraint, and resolve. Its reactions include Frozen, Melt, and Superconduct, which reduces enemiesâ€™ physical resistance.

Electro

Electro, governed by the Raiden Shogun of Inazuma, symbolizes energy, eternity, and discipline. It excels at sustained damage and reaction chains through Electro-Charged, Overloaded, Superconduct, and Dendro-based reactions.

Hydro

Hydro is associated with Focalors, the Hydro Archon of Fontaine, and represents justice, adaptability, and flow. It enables versatile reactions such as Vaporize, Frozen, Electro-Charged, and Bloom.

Anemo

Anemo, ruled by Barbatos of Mondstadt, embodies freedom, movement, and change. Its Swirl reaction spreads elemental effects across enemies and focuses on crowd control and amplification.

Geo

Geo is led by Morax of Liyue and symbolizes stability, contracts, and endurance. Through Crystallize, Geo creates elemental shields and emphasizes defense and consistency.

Dendro

Dendro, associated with Kusanali of Sumeru, represents wisdom, growth, and life. It enables complex reactions such as Bloom, Hyperbloom, Burgeon, Aggravate, and Spread.
